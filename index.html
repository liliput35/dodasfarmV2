Doda's Farm

About

Welcome to Doda's Farm where the waters meet the mountains & the plains. A beautiful farmhouse surrounded by lush greens & great scenery where everyday can feel like summer when you're enjoying a cool drink under the shade of our Indian Mango tree or by the pool.

Located in Binalbagan, Doda's Farm offers you amenities to enjoy your stay here. It includes a cozy loft complete with a bedroom and bathroom. The second floor is perfect for sleepovers good for 9 adults. Doda's Farm also has a pool good for both children and adults with stunning colorful LED lights that brings the place alive at night. There are also a few lounging areas surrounding the farmhouse that's good for reading books or chilling with loved ones. Music can be played at any time of the day as the place is equipped with a bluetooth speaker. Guests may also avail of sports equipment such as a football and a frisbee to play with in the Farm's spacious grassy areas.

Pool

Aside from the amenities Doda's Farm has to offer, you'll never get tired of the sky on all four directions from sunrise until sunset with a good view of Mt. Kanlaon and numerous mountain ranges. A clear view of the night sky from the Farm also offers opportunities of stargazing. Visit Doda's Farm now and experience a life away from the noises of the city.
